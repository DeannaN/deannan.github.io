About Deanna

Deanna has been a dancer all of her life with a background in jazz, ballet, modern, lyrical, jitterbug, ballroom and country line dancing. She began instructing country line and ballroom in the suburbs of Philadelphia in 1998 and was mentored by Carolyn Rollins of Carolyn and Company Dance Productions. She later performed as an apprentice with the ContempraDANCE Theatre Company in Wayne, Pennsylvania. After moving back to her hometown on the North Shore of Long Island a few years ago, some local friends requested Deanna teach a country line dancing class so they could give it a try before heading out in public to dance. She has been teaching locally and building the confidence of her students ever since! Hope to see you on the dance floor!
